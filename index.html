Slow down. Notice brush, glaze, and light. Our curated selection highlights the craft and subtlety of painting.

About the Exhibit

Pure Paintings brings together timeless, museum-quality works (public-domain and royalty-free) so the viewer can focus on composition, color, and technique. This online gallery is designed like a quiet gallery room: paper-white walls, soft spacing, and large reproductions to invite contemplation.
